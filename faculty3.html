Biography

Professor Chakrabarty is currently working as a Professor of Computer Science and Engineering at Brac University. His previous job was at the Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB), where he was in charge of the IT infrastructures. Professor Chakrabarty is serving as a jury member of various national IT competitions as well as serving as a reviewer of various international conferences and journals.
